Events organised by NSS NSUT CELL

The National Service Scheme (NSS) NSUT Cell is dedicated to fostering a spirit of service and social responsibility among students. Throughout the year, we organize a variety of events aimed at community development, awareness campaigns, and social outreach. These events not only provide valuable experiences for participants but also contribute significantly to the welfare of society. From health camps and environmental initiatives to educational workshops and cultural festivals, our events aim to engage students in meaningful service activities. Each event is an opportunity to learn, grow, and make a positive impact on the community, reflecting our commitment to building a more compassionate and responsible society.
