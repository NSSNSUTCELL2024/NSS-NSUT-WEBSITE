Positions of Responsibility (POR) in NSS

The Positions of Responsibility (POR) within the NSS (National Service Scheme) at NSUT are critical roles held by dedicated students who lead various initiatives and programs aimed at community service and social development. These positions include leadership roles such as the President and Vice President, as well as coordinators for administration, management, graphic design, content and social media. Each member brings unique skills and passion, working collaboratively to organize events, engage with the community, and promote social awareness among peers. Through their commitment and teamwork, they not only contribute to the NSS's mission but also foster a spirit of volunteerism and social responsibility within the university.
