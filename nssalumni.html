Alumni's of NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) fosters a spirit of service and community engagement among students. Our alumni have embraced the values of NSS, becoming leaders and changemakers in their communities. Through hands-on projects and initiatives, they have contributed to social development, environmental sustainability, and public health. NSS alumni are recognized for their commitment to volunteerism, showcasing the impact of service on personal growth and professional success. This alumni network serves as a platform for collaboration, mentorship, and continued involvement, ensuring that the spirit of service thrives beyond campus. Together, we celebrate their achievements and inspire future generations to make a difference.
